Algalon transforms the arena into space and immediately begins attacking the player with the highest threat. Algalon hits very hard and fast; he casts Quantum Strike on the tank with high frequency, so healers must ensure that the tank remains at full health at all times. All DPS merely attack the boss the best they can. Algalon can be positioned anywhere, as he has no frontal cleave or cone attacks.

Welcome to Astralis

#ToTheStars

The theme style was inspired by the Heroes of the Storm user interface, and a variety of Blizzard artstyles such as the Algalon the Observer bossfight in WotLK. The name 'Astralis' is a CS:GO organization. The reason I wanted to make something like this is because it looks shiny and eyecatching and I usually used super basic and simple bootstrap themes with 1-2 colors in the Angular6/Reactjs projects. Also wanted to impress the pretty lecturer girl. She is best girl.
